The Tata Group is a global conglomerate of companies based in India that was founded in 1868 by Jamsetji Tata. The group has more than 100 operating companies across six continents and ten business sectors, including automobiles, chemicals, consumer products, energy, engineering, financial services, information systems, materials, and telecommunications.
